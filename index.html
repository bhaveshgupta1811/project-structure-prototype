💰 DYNAMIC PRICING & ENROLLMENT LOGIC

Difficulty: Medium-Hard
Discount calculation, price changes anytime, price lock at purchase. Risk: Price inconsistency leads to revenue loss.

📄 SECURE PDF CONTENT DELIVERY

Difficulty: Medium
Prevent unauthorized PDF access, only enrolled users can download. Risk: Content piracy leads to revenue loss.
